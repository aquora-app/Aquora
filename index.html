1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Aquora collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

Sign In with Apple

If you choose to sign in, we collect your Apple User ID, name, and email address provided by Apple. Your Apple User ID is stored securely in the device Keychain. Your name and email are stored locally on your device only.

Screen Time Data

With your explicit permission, we access device usage data through Apple's Screen Time API to provide usage tracking and app limiting features. This data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted externally.

User Preferences & Progress

Settings such as your selected app limits, meditation preferences, quiz progress, reward tokens, and streak data are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and App Groups.

User-Generated Content

Journal entries and meditation notes you create are stored locally on your device. We do not access, read, or transmit this content.

Subscription Information

Subscription and tip purchase status is managed through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not have access to your payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide screen time tracking and reporting
  • To enforce app usage limits you have configured
  • To track your meditation and quiz progress
  • To manage your reward tokens and achievements
  • To generate personalized quiz questions via AI
  • To deliver a personalized experience

4. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. Data shared between the main app and extensions (Shield, Device Activity Monitor) uses secure App Group containers.

iCloud Sync

Token balance, subscription status, and transaction history may be synced via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store to restore your data across devices or after reinstallation. This data is encrypted by Apple's iCloud infrastructure. You can disable iCloud sync in your device settings.

Keychain

Your Apple User ID is stored in the device Keychain with encryption and is not accessible to other apps.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In — for authentication (Apple User ID verification)
  • Apple StoreKit — for subscription and tip purchase management
  • Apple Family Controls — for screen time management
  • Apple iCloud — for syncing token and subscription data
  • Google Gemini API — for generating quiz questions. Only your selected question categories and device language are sent. No personal data, screen time data, or identifiable information is transmitted.

6. Tracking & Advertising

Aquora does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not collect your device advertising identifier (IDFA). We do not track your activity across other apps or websites. The App does not request App Tracking Transparency permission because no tracking occurs.

7. Notifications

With your permission, Aquora sends local notifications to remind you about usage limits and challenges. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any remote push notification servers.

8. Children's Privacy

Aquora does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App's screen time management features may be used by parents to manage their children's device usage through Family Controls. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data is deleted when you uninstall the App. Data synced to iCloud (token balance, subscription status) may persist in your iCloud account until you manually remove it from Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage. We do not retain any data on external servers.
