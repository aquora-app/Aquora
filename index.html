2. Description of Service

Aquora is a screen time management and mindfulness application that helps users build healthier digital habits. Features include:

  • App Limits & Group Management — group apps and configure daily/session limits
  • Screen Time Tracking — usage statistics and session history
  • Unlocking Quiz Challenges — answer AI-generated quiz questions across 10 categories to unlock blocked apps
  • Meditation Sessions — guided breathing, focus, body scan, gratitude, and manifestation practices
  • Ambient Sounds — selectable nature and noise sounds for meditation
  • Journal & Manifestation — personal entries with goals and mood tracking
  • Personality Archetype System — 4-axis personality assessment with monthly reports
  • AI In-the-Moment Reminders — personalized usage reminders based on your behavior patterns
  • No-Unlocking Challenges — commit to not unlocking specific apps for set durations
  • Achievements & Rewards — badge system for streaks, time saved, and consistent practice
  • Token System — earn virtual tokens through challenges, redeemable for premium access
  • Referral Program — invite friends with codes for mutual rewards
  • Shareable Cards — export personality and achievement cards as images
  • Tip Jar — optional tips to support development

Free vs Premium

Some features have free-tier limits (e.g., 3 app limit groups, 3 quiz categories, 3 meditation sessions, 5 journal entries). Premium subscription unlocks all features without limits.

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Aquora collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

Sign In with Apple

If you choose to sign in, we collect your Apple User ID, name, and email address provided by Apple. Your Apple User ID is stored securely in the device Keychain. Your name and email are stored locally on your device only.

Screen Time Data

With your explicit permission, we access device usage data through Apple's Screen Time API and DeviceActivity framework to provide usage tracking, app limiting, and shield features. This data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted externally.

User Preferences & Progress

Settings such as your selected app limits, meditation preferences, ambient sound choices, quiz progress, reward tokens, achievements, and streak data are stored locally using UserDefaults and App Groups.

User-Generated Content

Journal entries, manifestation goals, mood tracking, and meditation notes you create are stored locally on your device. We do not access, read, or transmit this content.

Behavior Logs

To power personalized features (AI Reminders, Personality Archetype, monthly reports), the App stores the following behavior logs locally with a 90-day retention period:

  • Quiz answers (question ID, category, correctness, response time, hour of day, app context)
  • Reflection responses (selected options and free-text answers, app context, timestamp)
  • App unlock events (app group name, unlock method, optional user-stated reason, day/hour)

This data stays on your device and is used only for local analysis.

Personality Archetype Data

Your responses to the personality questionnaire and your derived 4-letter archetype code are stored locally. Monthly archetype snapshots are kept for trend tracking. To compute rarity statistics, an anonymized record (random device UUID + your archetype code only) is uploaded to Apple's CloudKit public database. This record contains no identifying information.

Referral Information

When you generate or redeem a referral code, the code along with SHA-256 hashed identifiers is stored in Apple's CloudKit public database to validate redemptions and award rewards. We never store plaintext identifiers in CloudKit.

Subscription Information

Subscription and tip purchase status is managed through Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not have access to your payment information or credit card details.

Photo Library (Optional)

If you choose to save or share personality cards or achievement badges, the App requests permission to write images to your Photo Library. We do not read existing photos.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide screen time tracking and usage reports
  • To enforce app usage limits and shield blocked apps
  • To track your meditation, journal, and quiz progress
  • To manage reward tokens, achievements, and streaks
  • To generate personalized quiz questions via AI
  • To deliver in-the-moment AI reminders based on your usage patterns
  • To compute your personality archetype and produce monthly reports
  • To validate referral codes and award referral rewards
  • To detect habit patterns and suggest helpful interventions

4. Data Storage & Security

The vast majority of user data is stored locally on your device. Data shared between the main app and extensions (Shield, Device Activity Monitor, Shield Action) uses secure App Group containers.

iCloud Key-Value Store

Token balance, subscription status, and transaction history may be synced via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store to restore your data across devices or after reinstallation. This data is encrypted by Apple's iCloud infrastructure. You can disable iCloud sync in your device settings.

CloudKit Public Database

Two types of records are stored in our CloudKit public database:

  • ReferralCode — referral code string with hashed (SHA-256) owner and redeemer identifiers
  • ArchetypeUser — anonymous device UUID paired with your personality code, used to calculate archetype rarity (capped at 400 records per category)

These records contain no personally identifiable information.

Keychain

Your Apple User ID is stored in the device Keychain with encryption and is not accessible to other apps.
